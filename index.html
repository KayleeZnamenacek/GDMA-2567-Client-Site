Our Process

We have a constantly evolving, hand picked selection of fine coffee origins and delicious blends engineered to help you brew a cafe-quality cup at home. Our coffee is roasted in a hot air, refractory lined roaster with an extraordinary level of control. Most small coffee roasters use a single stage burner which offers virtually no control during the roasting process.

The significance of relying on a high volume of hot air to roast coffee beans can’t be understated. The refractory significantly reduces hot spots which can damage coffee beans and produces a much more even roasting environment.
